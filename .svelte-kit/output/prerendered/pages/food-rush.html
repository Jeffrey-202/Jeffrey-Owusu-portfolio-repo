Food Rush Game

Food Rush

Food Rush is a game that I've I created for a UI programming project.

The aim of the game is to collect as much food as you can. When you get hit by 4 boulders it's game over and you restart.

For mobile devices you can use the left and right buttons on the screen to move the character and for desktop devices you can use the left and right arrow keys on your keyboard
